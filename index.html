Use code to solve problems

We program using the popular and powerful language of Java to solve complex problems competitively. Whether you're a wizard at coding, or still a Java novice, we have a place for you here to pursue your interest in programming with other like-minded individuals.

( It is recommended that you have completed or are currently in Computer Science A )

Compete With Others

As our main focus of the club, we engage in competitions both over the internet and travel in person to compete against other programmers.

Improve your programming skills

Our goal is to create an environment that promotes learning and the exchange of ideas between fellow programmers. Whether you're an expert or just a beginner, everyone can learn new skills and advance their coding abilities through the sport of coding competitively. From daily practice in each meeting to mentoring and lessons from our captains, learning is key.
